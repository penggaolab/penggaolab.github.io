Gao
Research Group


The Gao Research Group is at the Department of Environmental and Occupational Health at the University of Pittsburgh School of Public Health. We apply mass spectrometry-based targeted and non-targeted analyses to determine the environmental contaminants and xenobiotics in environmental and biological samples. We also utilize next- and third-generation sequencing approaches to investigate the microbiome profiles in these samples. Our long-term goal is to integrate the exposomics with multi-omics profiles and investigate how the exposome impacts human health, as well as better understand the etiology of chronic and idiopathic diseases.
